Asset Policy

All assets must meet the following standards:

  • Textures below 1024x1024 unless necessary
  • Models optimized under 3000 triangles
  • Animations under 2MB
  • No copyrighted audio
  • Consistent naming structure

UI Guidelines

Our UI is based heavily on gradients, glass effects, soft borders, and minimalistic typography. Consistency keeps our games recognizable and clean.

Monetization Strategy

UDGAMES uses fair monetization that does not force players to pay but gives valuable benefits. We avoid pay-to-win systems.
